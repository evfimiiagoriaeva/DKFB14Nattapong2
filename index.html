Reheating Grilled Steak with Au Jus

Description

Ingredients 2 pound leftover grilled steak 1-1/4 pints beef broth 1-1/2 tablespoons butter 2 tablespoons black vinegar

Cooking

1

Grill the steaks you can see them far left on grill. Eat what you want then you may have leftovers. Melt butter in a skillet.

2

Add the steak when butter is melted. Turn to coat a bit of butter on both sides. No add the broth, turn to medium heat turning occasionally but don't neglect them. Add in the black vinegar.

3

Simmer till reduced. Now turn often. Now they are done. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
